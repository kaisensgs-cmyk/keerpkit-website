About KeeprKit Smart Beehive

KeeprKit is a low-cost agricultural technology solution built to support farmers and modernize beekeeping in India. Designed specifically for Indian honey bees, it improves hive management, increases productivity, and supports sustainable agriculture.

Challenges in Traditional Beekeeping

Slow Honey Extraction

Traditional methods require 4 to 6 hours per hive and disturb bee colonies.

Lack of Hive Monitoring

No real-time data on temperature, humidity, or hive activity leads to delayed action.

Pest and Mite Attacks

Varroa mites, ants, and wasps damage colonies and reduce honey yield.

KeeprKit Smart Hive Solution

Flow Frame Technology

Enables easy honey extraction without cutting the comb, reducing stress on bees.

IoT-Based Monitoring

Sensors track temperature, humidity, and hive activity with real-time alerts.

AI Pest Detection System

Camera-based system detects harmful mites and protects the hive.
